Us

September 22, 2012


In an attempt to make her Orchestra party a little bit more appealing, my college roommate (and now bridesmaid!) Dana, decided to extend the invitation to friends and anyone else we knew. Because yes, you're right, an Orchestra party doesn't sound all that poppin'.

So call it what you want - serendipity, fate, or a Colorado guy on the hunt for some smart ladies - but Mike ended up at that party in my college apartment. After the party wound down, we stumbled over to 1020 (a local bar), and flirted some more. We exchanged numbers, texted regularly, and by the following week I was visiting his place in Brooklyn and meeting his dog.

As our story evolves and moves from one Manhattan apartment to another, with our dog J in toe, I have to say that I've never been more grateful for being at the right place at the right time.

We're so fortunate to have all of you as part of our story one way or another, and can't wait to celebrate and drink up at the party of all parties, OUR WEDDING!
