Directions


FROM WEST TO WEDDING

  • Take I-495 E/Long Island Expy toward Eastern Long Island (the bulk of the trip)
  • Take exit 70 toward NY-27/Alt Route/Montauk
  • Use the right 2 lanes to turn right onto County Rd 111 (signs for Eastport/Montauk)
  • Continue onto County Rd 111/Port Jefferson-Westhampton Beach Hwy
  • Merge onto NY-27 E
  • Take exit 63S for County Rd 31 South toward Westhampton Beach
  • Merge onto County Rd 31/Old Riverhead Rd
  • Turn left onto County Rd 80 E/Montauk Hwy
  • Turn right onto Beach Rd
  • Continue onto Beach Ln
  • Turn left onto Dune Rd
  • Venue will be on the right, parking will be on the left! (follow signs)

FROM WEST TO HOTEL

  • Take I-495 E/Long Island Expy toward Eastern Long Island (approx 60 miles)
  • Use the left lane to take exit 73 toward Greenport/Orient/Suffolk County 58
  • Keep left and merge onto Old Country Rd
  • Merge onto Old Country Rd
  • Turn right after 0.7 miles, your destination will be on the left!

FROM HOTEL TO WEDDING

    Head North told Old Country Road
  • Turn right at the 1st cross street onto Old Country Rd
  • Turn right onto Mill Rd
  • Turn left onto W Main St
  • Turn right onto Center Dr
  • Use the left lane to turn slightly right onto Nugent Dr
  • Slight left to stay on Nugent Dr; then keep right to stay on Nugent Dr
  • At the traffic circle, take the 2nd exit and continue onto Riversleigh Ave
  • Continue onto Quogue Riverhead Rd
  • At the traffic circle, take the 2nd exit and stay on Quogue Riverhead Rd
  • Turn right onto County Rd 80 W
  • Slight left onto Quogue St
  • Turn left onto Post Ln
  • At the traffic circle, continue straight to stay on Post Ln
  • Turn right onto Dune Rd
  • Venue will be on the left, parking will be on the right! (follow signs)
