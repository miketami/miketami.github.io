The Proposal


When I decided I wanted to propose to Tami, I knew it had to be at the first place we went to, on the first night we met, our bar; 1020. The only question was, when? Once my jeweler finished making the ring, I knew that I had to get rid of that thing before it burned a hole in my pocket.

First step, call Tami's mom, Millie. She was, of course, ecstatic. Then I needed to call Tami's best friends, close co-workers, and some of my friends. In less than 48 hours from the point of my decision to propose, we had to devise an elaborate plan in which I would take Tami out to dinner, while our friends and family would be gathering at 1020 with flowers, balloons, and a lot of anxiety, waiting for the big surprise.

After dinner and a bottle of dope wine, Tami and I arrived outside the bar, as planned, and EVERYONE - from the bridesmaids to the busboy - were watching us from inside the bar. Tami's wonderful sister, Crystal, recorded the whole thing and trust me, the video says it all...
